About CJCHT

Since 1989, CJCHT has been a certified women's business enterprise dedicated to delivering exceptional quality and service. Our legacy spans over three decades of innovation, craftsmanship, and unwavering commitment to excellence in the footwear industry.

Our Story

Founded as a women-owned business enterprise, CJCHT has grown from a small footwear manufacturer to a globally recognized brand. Our journey began with a simple vision: to create high-quality footwear that combines traditional craftsmanship with modern innovation.

Over the years, we have built strong relationships with partners across Asia, Europe, and North America, establishing ourselves as a trusted name in the industry. Our commitment to excellence extends beyond our products to encompass our values of sustainability, cultural engagement, and community building.

Our Mission

We strive to create exceptional footwear that not only meets the highest standards of quality and comfort but also reflects our commitment to environmental responsibility and social impact. Through innovative design, sustainable practices, and meaningful partnerships, we aim to make a positive difference in the world.

Core Values

Quality Excellence

Every product we create reflects our unwavering commitment to superior craftsmanship and attention to detail.

Sustainable Innovation

We continuously seek new ways to minimize our environmental impact while maximizing positive change.

Cultural Engagement

Through initiatives like the Boven Magazine Library, we foster community connections and cultural enrichment.

Global Partnership

Our worldwide network enables us to deliver localized solutions while maintaining universal standards.

Leadership

Our leadership team brings together decades of experience in footwear manufacturing, sustainable business practices, and global market development. As a certified women's business enterprise, we are proud to champion diversity and inclusion in everything we do.

Global Presence

From our showroom in Putian to international partnerships across Asia, Europe, and North America, CJCHT maintains a truly global presence. Our international network allows us to understand local markets while delivering consistent quality and service worldwide.
